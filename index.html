Everything you need to know about HashAPP™

The genetic database is the practical foundation of HashAPP™. It's a powerful tool that serves as the core of our platform, offering:
  • Comprehensive genetic library tracking
  • Analysis of strains with matching or intersecting lineages
  • Identification of similar strains based on aggregated data
  • Insights derived above anylysis methods or growth patterns, yields, strain tags & more!
This database enables you to make data-driven decisions, optimize your cultivation processes, and discover new potential in your genetic library.

HashAPP™ offers a comprehensive suite of tools to maximize your yield potential, including:
  • Advanced genetic tracking to identify and optimize top-performing strains.
  • In-depth yield analysis for uncovering trends and actionable insights.
  • Efficient facility management tools to streamline operations.
  • Customizable reports for monitoring progress and making informed decisions.
Leverage these resources to make data-driven decisions, refine your cultivation methodology and find proper extraction candidates. Doing this, you can expect to achieve consistently higher yields, and enhance the efficiency of your solventless operations.
